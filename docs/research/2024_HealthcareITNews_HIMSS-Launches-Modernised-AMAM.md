## Summary

The HIMSS Analytics Maturity Assessment Model (AMAM) was updated and officially launched in October 2024 at the 2024 HIMSS APAC Health Conference & Exhibition. The modernized model, "AMAM24", focuses on five key imperatives:
1. Governance
2. Analytics life cycle
3. Data and infrastructure
4. Personalized analytics
5. Analytics impact and outcomes

## Key Statistics

*   **Stage 6:** Globally, **26 organizations** have achieved Stage 6.
*   **Stage 7:** Globally, **13 organizations** have achieved Stage 7.

## Context

The update aims to help health systems keep pace with evolving analytics technologies and AI, moving beyond basic reporting to predictive and prescriptive capabilities.
