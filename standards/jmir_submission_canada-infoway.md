# How do I submit to the Canada Infoway e-collection?

**Source:** https://support.jmir.org/hc/en-us/articles/360018638552-How-do-I-submit-to-the-Canada-Infoway-e-collection

---

**IMPORTANT:** Effective Aug 5, 2025 the Canada Infoway-JMIR partnership has been paused pending a program review.

## Eligibility

If your project was funded by Canada Health Infoway and it was written up as a paper with some evaluation component, then the Article Processing Fee may be covered by our partnership with Infoway.

## Submission Details

- Papers will be published in the Infoway e-collection
- Most of the papers funded by Infoway fit into JMIR Med Inform, although they can also be submitted to other journals
- When submitting, please select the Infoway section

## How to Submit

1. Follow the instructions at "How do I submit to a theme issue?"
2. Submissions must be invited by Canada Health Infoway
3. Please ensure that your cover letter mentions this approval and provide proof of the invitation (this can be a formal letter or a copy of email correspondence)
4. If you have questions about proof of invitation, please contact ed-support@jmir.org
5. Select the "Canada Health Infoway" section in step 1 of the submission process

## Fees

- No submission fee/APF will be charged; JMIR bills Infoway directly
- Infoway also supports Visual Abstracts, so please check that option when checking out
