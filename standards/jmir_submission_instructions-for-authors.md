# Where are your instructions for authors?

**Source:** https://support.jmir.org/hc/en-us/articles/115001339607-Where-are-your-instructions-for-authors

---

Instructions for Authors can be found on the website of each journal.

## How to Find Instructions

1. Click on "Journal Information" in the top banner beside the journal name
2. Choose "Author Information" from the drop down menu

## Key Resources

- **Full-length submission preparation checklist:** Submitting Your Manuscript to JMIR Publications: A Guide for Authors
  - The submission preparation checklist is primarily applicable to article types that contain original research data

- **JMIR Publications Author Hub and Knowledge Base:** For more information for authors
