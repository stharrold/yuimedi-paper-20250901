# Ordering of Multiple Authors

**Source:** https://support.jmir.org/hc/en-us/articles/115001327087-Ordering-of-Multiple-Authors

---

In the case of multiple authors, the authors can be ordered using the "Move Author Up" and "Move Author Down" buttons found at the bottom of the individual Author submission form.

## How to Reorder Authors

- **Clicking "Move Author Up"** will place move the author position up one level switching place with the author above
- **Clicking "Move Author Down"** will place move the author position up one level switching place with the author above

This change will automatically be saved to your submission. There is no need to resave the author for it to take effect.
