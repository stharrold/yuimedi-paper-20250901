# How do I upload/add supplementary files (figures, Multimedia Appendix, additional material for reviewers/editors only) after submission?

**Source:** https://support.jmir.org/hc/en-us/articles/360001218371-How-do-I-upload-add-supplementary-files-figures-Multimedia-Appendix-additional-material-for-reviewers-editors-only-after-submission

---

You can add supplementary files at any time during the submission process.

## How to Add Supplementary Files

1. Navigate to the submitted article by going to your author homepage (e.g. http://www.jmir.org/author)
2. Click on the article title
3. Click on the link "add supplementary file"
