# Do you use American or British English spelling?

**Source:** https://support.jmir.org/hc/en-us/articles/115001433771-Do-you-use-American-or-British-English-spelling

---

We copyedit our manuscripts according to the AMA Style Guide, which means that we use American spelling.

When in doubt, please consult Merriam-Webster's dictionary.
