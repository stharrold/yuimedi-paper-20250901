# Do I need to be a member in order to submit my manuscript?

**Source:** https://support.jmir.org/hc/en-us/articles/115001062011-Do-I-need-to-be-a-member-in-order-to-submit-my-manuscript

---

No, it is not necessary to have a membership (individual or institutional) before submitting to our journals.

Some memberships do offer Article Processing Fee waivers. Please check out our memberships page for more details.
