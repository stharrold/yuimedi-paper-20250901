# How do I delete an author from the metadata?

**Source:** https://support.jmir.org/hc/en-us/articles/360015617332-How-do-I-delete-an-author-from-the-metadata

---

Sometimes one author is accidentally added to the metadata twice.

## How to Delete

You can delete a duplicate author using the "Delete Author" button at the bottom of an author's affiliation information.

This change can be made by an author at any time following submission and before publication.
